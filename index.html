Online Word Counter Tool

Word Counter is a free online tool that instantly counts words, characters (with and without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs in your text. It also estimates reading time (based on 275 WPM) and speaking time (based on 150 WPM). Perfect for essays, blog posts, social media, and more.

Features

  • Real-time word, character, sentence, and paragraph counting
  • Reading and speaking time estimation
  • Text case conversion (uppercase, lowercase, title case)
  • Works entirely in your browser

All processing runs locally in your browser. No data is sent to any server.
